GDG DevFest Family Hackathon

Follow us on GitHub

Join us at GDG DevFest

Kids ages 8-14 welcome. Adult kids welcome too.

Come out for an AM or PM 2.5 hour exploration sprint, with partners like Sphero, the maker of the newest droid in the Star Wars family!

Lunch and programming activities will be provided. Lunch will be pizza, with vegetarian options available but no vegan or gluten free options planned (sorry, please plan ahead if that is a requirement).

All children must be accompanied by a supervising adult.

Remember what it was like to program and feel like this for the first time or all over again!

Tickets

Due to space considerations, this event will be ticketed. See the Eventbrite page for more info.

Please pay special attention to what hours your ticket is for. Participants will only be allowed into the exploration area during their designated times. If you are a parent and two kids looking for a morning ticket, you should buy 3 morning tickets and plan to have an awesome time from 10:00AM until 12:30PM with lunch during that last 30 minutes, for example.

We are also seeking mentor-coaches who can bring their own projects that are kid friendly (like the LED sign pictured) or come ready to be trained in one of our partner's technologies. Volunteers should plan to commit to a full day from 9:30 - 3:30.

Morning attendees will have lunch from 12 - 12:30, afternoon attendees will have lunch 12:30 - 1PM.

Note: Orders that do not complete order information correctly or attempt to circumvent the 8 ticket limit may be cancelled.
